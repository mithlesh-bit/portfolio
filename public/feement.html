Feement the fee management solutions

Project information

  • Category: Web design
  • Name: Feement
  • Project date:december 2022

Feement is a web application designed to efficiently manage the fee structure of a college. The platform incorporates comprehensive details of students, including their semester-wise payment records. Utilizing this application, college administrators can easily track and update fee payments, allowing for seamless monitoring of students' financial transactions and academic progress.
